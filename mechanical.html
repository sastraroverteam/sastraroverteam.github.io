The Mechanical Team

We design every mechanical structure which is to be found on the rover. To handle all these tasks we have divided ourselves further into three sub-teams:
  • Wheel team : They make the wheels after alot of number crunching.
  • Chassis team : They make the frame and body of the rover.
  • Suspension team : They design the suspension system for the rover.
