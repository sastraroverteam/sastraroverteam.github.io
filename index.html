What we do

Our main mission involves the design and development of rovers.
We brainstorm, build, code and have fun along the way.

Coding

We are comfortable in C, C++, Python, JavaScript and Java. We make use of TensorFlow, OpenCV, NumPy and many more libraries.
All of our code can be found here.

Wireless communications

We currently operate in the 2.4 GHz WiFi bands, but in the future hope to have our own custom solutions at other frequencies.

Wheels

After hours of number crunching, we have finalised our own custom made wheels for navigation on tricky and difficult terrain with relative ease.

Simulations

Using state-of-the-art systems provided to us by TBI, we have designed and tested both our rover's chassis and suspension as well as the neural networks for autonomous navigation.
