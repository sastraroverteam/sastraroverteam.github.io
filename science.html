The Science Team

We mix compounds in test tubes and through colourful reactions, we deem the life sustainability of a given soil sample. To handle all these tasks we have divided ourselves further into two sub-teams:
  • Testing Team : We get decide the tests which the rover will be performing.
  • Analysis Team : We are responsible for making the results human-readable.
